About us

This name reflects the desire of the owners of the restaurant, a Korean couple who moved to Spain back in 1989, to offer only the most delicious Korean cuisine to their guests. Now over 70 years old, they have finally been able to make their dream of running a restaurant come true in order to offer their guests the flavors of Korean cuisine from Jeolla Do province, the quintessential culinary province of Korea.
Bae Pepe

Our kitchen

Korean cuisine is different from Chinese cuisine and Japanese cuisine, better known in our country, and although it has common roots and characteristics, such as its use of soybeans and spices, it is quite different from them in ingredients, textures and flavors.
This also highlights the wok (roast) kitchen characterized by its curious kitchen utensils that allow the roast to be completed on your plate and maintain the maximum possible flavor.
